A production by [ArtesMobiles](https://www.artesmobiles.art) in co-production with Stadttheater Gießen, created as a prologue to the performance [_Versuch über die nachhaltige Ordnung merkwürdiger Dinge_](https://stadttheater-giessen.de/de/magazin/beitrag/wenn-das-buhnenbild-lebendig-wird/) (Essay on the Sustainable Order of Strange Things) within the [Fonds Zero](https://www.kulturstiftung-des-bundes.de/de/projekte/detail/zero_klimaneutrale_kunst_und_kulturprojekte.html) program of the Kulturstiftung des Bundes.

First launched in [Stadtheater Gießen](https://stadttheater-giessen.de) on November 1st, 2025 //[ more info](https://www.artesmobiles.art/produktionen/liebding) // [**try LiebDing**](https://liebding.art) !

![](/cms/images/blank.jpg)

> In the _LiebDing_ dating app, the audience in the foyer of the Stadttheater Gießen can get to know items from the prop collection in an intimate way. Interaction is made possible through chatbots: props, costumes, and furniture are given a voice and engage in conversation.
>
>_LiebDing_ is more than just a game. It is a bridge between technical innovation and human expertise. It is a tribute to the “invisible” people behind the scenes whose knowledge and passion keep the theater alive. Their anecdotes, expertise, and passion have been incorporated into the chatbots, breathing surprisingly authentic life into them. When you “match” with a prop, you're not just talking to an object, but also to the memory of the theater.
>
> The objects you write to here really exist! You will encounter them. 
Somewhere in the depths of the Giessen Stadttheater. In the storage rooms, the corridors, the archives.
