Concept, instrument design and construction, performing: **Lottie Sebes**

> Harness design and construction: **Anna Cingi**

An original performance by [Lottie Sebes](https://www.lottiesebes.com) with sewing machine motors, strings, microphones, electronics, talk box.

First performed at [Silent Green Kunstquartier](https://www.silent-green.net), Berlin on December 13th, 2025 // [more info](https://www.silent-green.net/en/programme/detail/2025/12/13/psychic-liberation-night-helm-lottie-sebes) // [teaser](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tkU-IGLtt8w)

![](/cms/images/blank.jpg)

> Sound artist and performer Lottie Sebes explores the sonic junction of music and machine, embracing the clunky aliveness of outmoded mechanisms. In her sonic performance Conduit, she uses an industrial sewing machine motor as the main sound source.  A self-built system of electromagnetic microphones, strings and modified sewing machine pedals allows Lottie to transform the motor’s electrical and physical movements into sound. 
>
> Sebes’s work is a laborious and erotic negotiation of control and resistance — a techno-somatic dialogue between human and mechanical vitality.
